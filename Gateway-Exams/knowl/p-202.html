Use substitution with \(u=5x^3-8\) and \(du=15x^2 \, dx\text{,}\) so \(\dfrac{1}{15} \, du = x^2 \, dx\text{:}\)

\begin{align*} \int x^2(5x^3-8)^4 \, dx \amp = \int \frac{1}{15} \cdot u^4 \, du\\ \amp = \frac{1}{15} \cdot \frac{u^5}{5} + C\\ \amp = \frac{(5x^3-8)^5}{75} + C \end{align*}
