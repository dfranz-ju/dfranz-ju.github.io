Solution 2.8.8.1.

Use substitution with \(u = t^2 + 5\) and \(du = 2t \, dt\text{,}\) so \(\dfrac{1}{2} \, du = t \, dt\text{:}\)

\begin{align*} \int 7t\cos(t^2+5) \, dt \amp = \int 7 \cdot \frac{1}{2} \cos(u) \, du\\ \amp = \frac{7}{2} \sin(u) + C\\ \amp = \frac{7}{2} \sin(t^2+5) + C \end{align*}
