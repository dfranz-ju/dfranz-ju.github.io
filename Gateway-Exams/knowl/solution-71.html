Solution 2.1.7.1.

Use substitution with \(u=x^4+5\) and \(du = 4x^3 \, dx\text{,}\) so \(\dfrac{1}{4} \, du = x^3 \, dx\text{.}\)

\begin{align*} \int 2x^3(x^4+5)^6 \, dx \amp = 2\int u^6 \cdot \frac{1}{4} \, du\\ \amp = \frac{1}{2} \frac{u^7}{7} + C\\ \amp = \frac{(x^4+5)^7}{14} + C \end{align*}
