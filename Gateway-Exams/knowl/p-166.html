Use substitution with \(u=4x+7\) and \(du = 4\, dx\text{,}\) so \(\dfrac{1}{4} \, du = dx\text{.}\)

\begin{align*} \int \sqrt{4x+7} \, dx \amp = \int u^{1/2} \cdot \frac{1}{4} \, du\\ \amp \frac{1}{4} \cdot \frac{u^{3/2}}{3/2} + C\\ \amp = \frac{1}{4}\cdot \frac{2}{3} (4x+7)^{3/2} +C \end{align*}
