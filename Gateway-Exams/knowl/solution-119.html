Solution 2.7.7.1.

Use substitution with \(u=121+x^2\) and \(du=2x \, dx\text{,}\) so \(\dfrac{1}{2} \, du = x \, dx\text{:}\)

\begin{align*} \int \frac{x}{(121+x^2)^2} \, dx \amp = \int \frac{1}{2} u^{-2} \, du\\ \amp = \frac{1}{2} \frac{u^{-1}}{-1} + C\\ \amp = -\frac{1}{2} \cdot (121+x^2)^{-1} + C \end{align*}
