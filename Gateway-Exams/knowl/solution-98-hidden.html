Use integration by parts with

\begin{align*} u \amp = t^2 \amp dv \amp = e^{t} \, dt\\ du \amp = 2t \, dt \amp v \amp = e^{t} \end{align*}

Then

\begin{align*} \int t^2e^{t} \, dt \amp = t^2\cdot e^{t} - \int e^{t} \cdot 2t \, dt\\ \amp =t^2 e^{t} - \int 2te^{t} \, dt \end{align*}

We now use integration by parts again on the new integral.

\begin{align*} u \amp = 2t \amp dv \amp = e^{t} \, dt\\ du \amp = 2\, dt \amp v \amp = e^{t} \end{align*}

So

\begin{align*} \int t^2e^{t} \, dt \amp = t^2 e^{t} - \left( 2t e^{t} - \int 2e^{t} \, dt\right)\\ \amp = t^2 e^{t} - 2te^{t} +2e^{t} + C \end{align*}
