The maximum population is at the beginning of July, which is \(t=6\text{:}\)

\begin{equation*} P(6) = -5\cos\left(\frac{\pi}{6} \cdot 6 \right) + 10 = -5(-1) +10 = 15 \text{ thousand rabbits}\text{.} \end{equation*}

The minimum population is at the beginning of January, which is \(t=0\text{:}\)

\begin{equation*} P(0) = -5\cos\left(\frac{\pi}{6} \cdot 0 \right) + 10 = -5(1) +10 = 5 \text{ thousand rabbits} \end{equation*}
