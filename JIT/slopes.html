Section 1.1 Lines and Slope

Checkpoint 1.1.1.

Find the slope of the line between the points \((2, 8)\) and \((-2, 12)\)

The slope is .

Answer.

\(-1\)

Solution.

The slope is \(\dfrac{12 - 8}{-2-2} = \dfrac{4}{-4} = {-1}\)

Checkpoint 1.1.2.

Find the slope of the line between the points \((12, 5)\) and \((13, 5)\)

The slope is .

Answer.

\(0\)

Solution.

The slope is \(\dfrac{5 - 5}{13-12} = \dfrac{0}{1} = {0}\)
