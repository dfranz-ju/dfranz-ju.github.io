Desmos: Below is another interactive visual demonstration of linear transformations using Desmos that lets you experiment with standard matrices and view the action of a matrix transformation on a square or an asymmetric shape. The file shows the images of \(\mathbf{e}_1\) and \(\mathbf{e}_2\) to further connect to the standard matrix idea. You can find the standard matrix of a particular transformation by dragging these images around and looking at the slider values of \(a,b,c,\) and \(d\text{.}\)
