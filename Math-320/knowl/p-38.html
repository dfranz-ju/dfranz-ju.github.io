Desmos: Below is an interactive visual demonstration of linear combinations using Desmos. The starting vectors are \(\mathbf{u}=(1,3)\) and \(\mathbf{v}=(-2,1)\text{.}\) The vectors \(c\mathbf{u}\) and \(d\mathbf{v}\) are shown in blue, while the linear combination \(c\mathbf{u} + d\mathbf{v}\) is shown in purple. You can use the sliders for \(c\) and \(d\) to adjust their values and see how the linear combination changes. You can hit the play symbol on each slider or manually drag the slider to change the value. (To view the Desmos file below in its own (larger) window, click here)
