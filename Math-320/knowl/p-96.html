Desmos: Below is an interactive visual demonstration of linear transformations using Desmos that lets you create similar images as those in the video. The base \(xy\)-grid is shown in dashed black and blue lines, and this grid transformed by the matrix \(\begin{bmatrix} a \amp b \\ c \amp d \end{bmatrix}\) is shown in purple and green. You can hit the play symbol on each slider or manually drag the slider to change the value of each entry of the matrix and create your own tranformation visuals. To animate the transformation, click play next to the "s" slider.
