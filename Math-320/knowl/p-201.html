In addition to mastering the basic objectives, here are the tasks you should be able to perform after class, with practice:

  • Interpret the coordinate mapping as a one-to-one and onto linear transformation.

  • Describe what an isomorphism of two vector spaces is.

  • Give examples of isomorphic vector spaces.

  • Use coordinate mappings to computationally answer questions about vector spaces other than \(\mathbb{R}^n.\)
