In addition to mastering the basic objectives, here are the tasks you should be able to perform after class, with practice:

  • Describe the solution set of a homogeneous system as the span of a set of vectors.

  • Describe solutions to linear systems in parametric vector form.

  • Use Theorem 6 to geometrically compare and contrast the solution sets of \(A\mathbf{x}=\mathbf{b}\) and \(A\mathbf{x}=\mathbf{0}\text{.}\)
