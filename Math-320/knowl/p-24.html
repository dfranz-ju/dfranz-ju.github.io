In addition to mastering the basic objectives, here are the tasks you should be able to perform after class, with practice:

  • Determine the solution set type (none, one, or infinite) of a linear system given its augmented matrix in echelon form.
  • Implement the row reduction algorithm to convert a matrix to echelon or reduced echelon form.
  • Find the general solution of a linear system and express it in parametric form.
